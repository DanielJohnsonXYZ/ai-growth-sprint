# Benchmark Research Notes

Date: 2026-04-22

## Benchmarks Reviewed

- Enterprise Nation: practical UK SME AI guidance and productivity framing.
- Xero UK: simple buyer language, business-type pages, trust and resource pathways.
- Tide UK: direct small-business CTAs and low-friction navigation.
- Simply Business: trust-led small-business tone and practical risk framing.

## Borrowed Patterns

- Make the first promise obvious: save time, improve output, stay in control.
- Create guides by category so cautious SME buyers can learn before booking.
- Use concrete workflows instead of generic "AI transformation" language.
- Add responsible-use boundaries as a conversion asset, not just legal copy.
- Keep proof honest where named testimonials/case studies are not yet permissioned.
